Mess Membership

All hostel inmates must be members of the mess without exception. No student is allowed to reside in the hostel without mess membership. In cases of excess demand for accommodation, students may be permitted mess facilities by paying full charges for the academic year, with the condition to join the hostel when accommodation is provided.

Application Process

Students seeking mess facility outside the hostel must submit a request form and pay the prescribed fee. Upon joining the mess, students must sign the mess joining register.

Mess Timings

Service hours are strictly between scheduled timings. The mess is closed after lunch every Saturday for maintenance.

Food System

Disposable self-service system is followed. Non-vegetarian food (chicken only) is served as an extra item on specified days.

Mess Boarding Charges (per academic year)

  • Mess Boarding Charges: 1,500.00
  • Administrative Charges:675.00
  • Hostel Magazine Fees: 50.00
  • Total: $2,225.00

*Note: The mess charges are applicable to all hostelites for one academic year, including those on scholarships.
